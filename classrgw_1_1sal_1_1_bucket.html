Bucket abstraction

This represents a bucket. A bucket is a container for objects. It is owned by a user, and has it's own set of metadata, including a set of key/value attributes. A bucket may not contain other buckets, only objects. Buckets have Access Control Lists (ACLs) that control what users can access the contents of the bucket, and in what ways.
