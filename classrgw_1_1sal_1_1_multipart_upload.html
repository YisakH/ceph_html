Abstraction of a multipart upload

This represents a multipart upload. For large objects, it's inefficient to do a single, long-lived upload of the object. Instead, protocols such as S3 allow the client to start a multipart upload, and then upload object in smaller parts in parallel. A MultipartUpload consists of a target bucket, a unique identifier, and a set of upload parts.
